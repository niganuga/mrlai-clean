Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ceramic coating last on a car?

Professional ceramic coating typically lasts 2-5 years depending on the quality and maintenance. Our premium ceramic coating comes with a 3-year warranty and can last up to 5 years with proper care.

Do you offer same day mobile detailing in Los Angeles?

Yes! We offer same-day service for Basic Wash and Premium Detail services. Luxury Treatment and ceramic coating require advance booking to ensure proper preparation and curing time.

How much does mobile car detailing cost in LA?

Our mobile car detailing prices start at $50 for a Basic Wash, $150 for Premium Detail, and $300 for Luxury Treatment. Ceramic coating ranges from $800-$1500 depending on your vehicle size and package selected.

What areas do you serve for mobile detailing?

We provide mobile car detailing services throughout Greater Los Angeles within a 50-mile radius, including Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, West Hollywood, Pasadena, Burbank, Glendale, and surrounding areas.

Do I need to provide water and electricity for mobile detailing?

No! We bring everything needed including our own water supply and generator. All we need is access to your vehicle and a parking space for our mobile unit.
